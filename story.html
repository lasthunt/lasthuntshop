Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Maldives' trusted fiber wheelbarrow manufacturer

Who We Are

Founded in 2014, LAST HUNT has grown from a small hardware shop to the leading manufacturer of fiber wheelbarrows in the Maldives. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide durable, high-quality tools that help our customers build better.

What started as a family-run business in Feeali has now expanded to serve customers across the Maldives, with our fiber wheelbarrows becoming the preferred choice for construction professionals in Malé and surrounding areas.

We take pride in our Maldivian roots and are committed to supporting local communities through job creation and skills development.

Our Manufacturing Process

Every LAST HUNT fiber wheelbarrow is crafted with precision and care, using only the highest quality materials and time-tested techniques.

1

Material Selection

We source premium fiberglass and reinforced polymers that withstand the harsh Maldivian climate and heavy-duty use.

2

Molding & Shaping

Our specialized molds create the perfect wheelbarrow shape, ensuring optimal weight distribution and durability.

3

Reinforcement

Critical stress points are reinforced with additional fiber layers for extra strength where it's needed most.

4

Quality Inspection

Each wheelbarrow undergoes rigorous testing for weight capacity, balance, and durability before leaving our facility.
